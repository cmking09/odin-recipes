Fried Chicken

Description

This is a recipe that will help you prepare the ancient meal of our ancestors, fried chicken.

It will be the exact way that Raising Canes does it.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Grease

Steps

  1. Kill a chicken
  2. Remove all feathers
  3. Drop it in a vat of boiling hot grease
  4. Remove chicken from vat of grease
  5. Finished!
