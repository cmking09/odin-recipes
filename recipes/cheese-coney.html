Skyline Coney

Description

This is a recipe to create one of the greatest culinary concoctions known to man, the cheese coney. The cheese coney is a staple in many different restarants in Cincinnatti and various other scattered regions. It is most well known for the chili that is placed on top of a hot dog.

The dish is beloved by few and reviled by many. Those who are fortunate enough to not engage in the barbarish hatred against it have their taste buds blessed with a delectable combination of chili, cheese, bun, and hot dog.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Hot Dog Weiner
  • Bun
  • Chili

Steps

  1. Cook the hot dog
  2. Cook the chili
  3. Remove the cheese from the fridge
  4. Remove a bun from the bag in the pantry
  5. Place hot dog on bun
  6. Place chili on hot dog
  7. Place cheese on Chili
  8. Finished!
